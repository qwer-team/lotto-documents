How to Play

Sweet Million


 
Steps to Winning Sweet Million
 
CHOOSE 6 NUMBERS OR ASK FOR A QUICK PICK
Simply ask for a Quick Pick or choose 6 numbers from a field of 1-40 on a Sweet Million playcard.
 
PLAY UP TO 10 GAMES OR 8 ADVANCE DRAWINGS ON ONE PLAYCARD
You can play up to 10 games on a single playcard or play up to 8 drawings in advance all on a single ticket.
 
PLAY DAILY
Play every day; on drawing nights, play until 9:00 p.m. Drawings are every Monday and Thursday at approximately 9:30 p.m.
