Game Highlights
  • Atlantic 49 is available only in Atlantic Canada
  • Jackpot is always $1 million
  • $1 per play for two sets of 6 numbers
  • Pick six numbers from 1 to 49 or choose Quick Pick
  • Draws are held every Wednesday and Saturday.
How to Play
  • Atlantic 49 is available only in Atlantic Canada.
  • For $1, you get 1 set of 6 numbers.
  • Play mulitple Atlantic 49 selections on one ticket.
  • Play Atlantic 49 by Quick Pick or Selection Slip.
  • Ask for a Quick Pick or select six numbers from a field of 1 to 49. 
  • Play Multiple Draws. Tell the Retailer how many draws you want or mark the number of draws on the Selection Slip.
