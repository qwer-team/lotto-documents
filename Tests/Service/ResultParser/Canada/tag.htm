Game Highlights
  • Atlantic Tag is available only in Atlantic Canada
  • If your TAG number matches the six-digit winning number in the exact order drawn, you win the $100,000 top prize! Other prizes are also offered for matching numbers right down to the last digit!
  • $1 per play
  • One six-digit number will be drawn at random from among all numbers from 100,000 to 699,999 every night
  • Draws are held every night of the week
  • Atlantic Tag is available on these lotteries:
    • Lotto 649
    • Lotto Max/Max Millions
    • Atlantic 49
    • Atlantic Pik4
    • Atlantic Keno
    • Atlantic Salsa Bingo
How to Play
  • Atlantic Tag is available only in Atlantic Canada.
  • For $1, you get 1 six digit number. Atlantic Tag numbers are always lottery computer generated for you
  • You can play Atlantic TAG up to 10 times on each ticket.
  • To play Atlantic Tag, ask your retailer for a selection slip for the main game you wish to play. Complete your selection slip like you normally do. For an additional $1, you can add Atlantic Tag to your ticket.
